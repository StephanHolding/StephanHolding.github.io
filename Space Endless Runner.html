Space Endless Runner VR

A virtual reality obstacle dodging game.
In space.

The controls

The ship is controlled by the rotation of one of the VR controllers. The rotation of the ship is represented by a holographic version of the ship inside the cockpit.

The rotation and movement

I used a special method for rotation. The ship itself is not flying, the obstacles are moving towards the ship. So I made an empty GameObject and let the ship look at it. Instead of rotating the ship directly, I moved the empty GameObject and so the rotated as well.

But the ship needed to move as well as rotate. So I created an axis representing the rotation of the controller. When the controller was held straight, the axis would be zero and the ship would obviously not rotate or move, but when the controller is held at an angle of 25 degrees, the axis would be 1.

This created very smooth and responsive rotation and movement.

Object Pooling

To maximize the FPS I used object pooling for the obstacles, instead of instantiating every asteroid seperately. Because a new asteroid has to be instantiated every second, or even less
